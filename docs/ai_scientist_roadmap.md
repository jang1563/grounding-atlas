# Roadmap to an ultimate AI scientist, and where our measurement layer fits

*2026-06-12. From a 5-dimension deep-research sweep (`ultimate-ai-scientist-roadmap` workflow; full sources in the workflow output). The single diagnosis, the layer-by-layer known-vs-missing, the bottleneck, the current position, and the next 3 moves. No em dashes.*

## The single diagnosis

Today's systems are strong ACCELERATION engines (hypothesis generation, literature grounding, interpolation over web-exposed knowledge) and unreliable DISCOVERY engines (extrapolation, causation, novelty verification). Every documented failure is one root cause: the system does not know when it is wrong.
- A-Lab "41 novel materials" (Nature 2023): an independent reanalysis (Palgrave/Schoop) found essentially none genuinely novel; the 2026 author correction conceded "novel" meant new-to-the-platform. Automated XRD/Rietveld analysis at "beginner, completely novice human level."
- Perturbation FMs lose to additive/no-change baselines (Ahlmann-Eltze, Nat Methods 2025); interventional <= observational on real single-cell data (CausalBench, Comms Biol 2025).
- AbstentionBench (NeurIPS 2025): abstention does NOT improve with scale, and reasoning-tuning makes it ~24% worse.
- Self-verification is near-zero (REPRO-Bench best 21.4%); the Wei survey (arXiv 2508.14111): "every evaluated framework produced sophisticated hallucinations," "no framework completed a full research cycle."
- ChemCrow (Nat Mach Intell 2024): LLM-as-judge cannot replace expert assessment, the evaluator "lacks the domain knowledge to distinguish fluent but incorrect answers from accurate ones."

## Known vs missing, by layer

- Strategy. KNOWN: the architecture is settled in shape (heterogeneous orchestrator + specialist FMs + verifier, not a bigger generalist); multi-agent ideation works (Google AI co-scientist, generate-debate-evolve + Elo). MISSING: no system closes literature -> execution -> validated, reproducible, genuinely-novel result. All wins are search/optimization (which catalyst, which repurposed drug), never new mechanism.
- Data / training-signal. KNOWN: RLVR where checkable; soft verification (rBio: reward from biological world models, SOTA on PerturbQA, parity with an experiment-trained ablation); interventional substrate being built (X-Atlas/Pisces 25.6M CRISPRi cells, PerturbQA). MISSING: signal for web-poor genuinely-novel science (RLVR sparse-reward-weakest there; soft verifiers are CIRCULAR, "cannot exceed their verifier," recover known effects not new biology); no machine-readable negative-results corpus at scale; sim-to-real validity of the reward is essentially unmeasured.
- Models. KNOWN: GPQA near-saturated, HLE ~20-41%; the negative results are reproducible and decisive (FMs lose to additive; interventional <= observational); AI-Feynman recovers clean physics but collapses above noise 0.01. MISSING: no model demonstrably extrapolates under intervention on non-additive/epistatic cases (the reported wins are additivity in disguise); causal representation learning is still identifiability theory + toy demos; SAE "mechanistic biology" is seed-unstable, not a reproducible object; a metric crisis (calibrated metrics may reverse the negative verdict, bioRxiv 2025.10.20.683304).
- Infrastructure. KNOWN: orchestration has wet-lab existence proofs (Virtual Lab nanobodies binding JN.1/KP.3, Nature 2025; Robin -> ripasudil for dry AMD, Nature 2026); MCP standardizing (BioinfoMCP 38 tools, 94.7%); Boltz-2 near-FEP at ~1000x cost = a cheap in-silico verifier. MISSING: nobody glued LLM-orchestrator + FM-verifier + autonomous bench into one autonomous cycle for a hard biology target; no measured verifier-reliability signal the orchestrator can condition on (so sim-as-reward is hackable); no benchmark scores the ORCHESTRATION DECISION (which tool, when, when to defer) as distinct from FM accuracy.
- Evaluation. KNOWN: dense task-solving layer (ScienceAgentBench, LAB-Bench, BixBench, DiscoveryBench, AstaBench 2400+; honest verdict "AI remains far from solving research"); grounding fidelity testable ("Lost in Tokenization" arXiv 2510.23127: scientific LLMs read the name/annotation not the raw content, adding raw sequence HURTS); calibration benchmarked (AbstentionBench). MISSING: no running benchmark scores a genuine novel-verifiable discovery (all grade against an existing paper, confounding recall with rediscovery); grounding fidelity exists in one domain only; calibration is not a scored outcome inside any discovery eval; causal-intervention validity has no standard benchmark (the "Virtual Cells as Causal World Models" axes are not yet a benchmark, and a mandatory linear/retrieval baseline is rarely enforced).

## The single biggest bottleneck

Trustworthy autonomous verification: the system reliably knowing when its own intermediate result is wrong. It is the A-Lab disaster directly, it is why the data layer is stuck (soft verifiers cannot exceed what they encode, oracle calibration unquantified), why infrastructure cannot close the loop (no verifier-reliability signal; REPRO-Bench 21%), and why evaluation is misleading (fit-to-data metrics cannot detect absent causal structure). Not a bigger model, and not a self-judging LLM (ChemCrow).

## Where the Bio_Grounding_Eval layer fits

Our measured contribution IS the instrument for the bottleneck. Placement (train/retrieve/orchestrate; train wins nowhere -> closed-weight models fine as orchestrators), grounding (encode >> verbalize, web-exposure-gated), and calibration (frontier = calibrated router, corr +0.90, routed ~ oracle) form the measurement substrate under the gate the whole loop must pass. The web-exposure law is the routing prior for verification-aware orchestration: the same finding that governs whether a model should answer a question governs whether an autonomous scientist should trust its own intermediate result. The ACCELERATION-vs-DISCOVERY framing is the field's fault line, now backed by CausalBench and Ahlmann-Eltze.

## The next 3 moves (all in our measurement lane, not competing on agents)

1. Causal grounding eval (highest leverage; pairs with the sibling CausalAtlas). Extend the grounding contract (encode-vs-verbalize, name-vs-content) from descriptive to causal/interventional: score held-out non-additive (epistatic) perturbations from anonymized content, with a MANDATORY additive/no-change/linear baseline so "beating correlation" is the bar. Operationalizes the Virtual-Cell causal axes that have no benchmark; turns the metric crisis into a settled question.
2. Calibration inside discovery. Promote our calibrated-router result to a risk-coverage / selective-prediction benchmark for discovery, where the correct move on an unfalsifiable or out-of-competence task is to abstain or hand off, the axis AbstentionBench shows scaling degrades, which no discovery eval scores. Converts +0.90 routing into the calibration layer the bottleneck demands.
3. Closed-loop placement probe (small, decisive). Wrap one cheap in-silico verifier (Boltz-2) behind an MCP contract and measure the ORCHESTRATION DECISION itself: when does the frontier router correctly trust the simulated reward versus escalate? Produces the measured verifier-reliability signal infrastructure lacks, scores the orchestration decision nobody scores, tests whether retrieve+orchestrate suffices on a causal/computation-bound task. Wet-lab out of scope (the in-silico half is enough).

Sequencing: Moves 1 and 2 are pure measurement extensions of our three existing results (low cost, publishable from current data); Move 3 bridges to infrastructure and sets up an eventual closed-loop collaboration. All three stay in the measurement + calibration + placement lane, rather than building agents.

## Hype vs real

REAL (load-bearing): FM-orchestration validated artifacts (Virtual Lab nanobodies, Robin ripasudil, co-scientist AML repurposing, all with human-executed back ends); Boltz-2 near-FEP at ~1000x; and the negative results, which are the most trustworthy datapoints in the corpus (FMs < additive baselines; interventional <= observational; abstention degrades with scale; REPRO 21%). Soft verification reaches PARITY with experimental data (rBio), not exceedance.

HYPE (discount): "autonomous discovery" (A-Lab is the cautionary tale; treat any "the AI discovered X" as needing independent human reanalysis); cherry-picked anecdotes as hit-rates (Robin n=1, Sakana's peer-review paper pre-arranged for withdrawal); "causal extrapolation" (mostly interpolation/additivity); "AI-validated"/self-verification (REPRO 21%, LLM-as-judge inadequate); SAE "mechanistic discovery" (seed-unstable).

One-line: current AI scientists are powerful hypothesis/draft generators with a human-validated or human-executed back end; the decisive missing capability is trustworthy autonomous verification of novelty and correctness, and that is precisely the gap our measurement + calibration + placement layer is positioned to instrument first.
